Since I use Google Analytics to track who visits the site, I am required to disclose the use of it. This is why there's a privacy policy on my footer. Here's the [support article](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/7318509?hl=en) for that.

For those who don't want to be tracked, use an adblocker! My personal favorite is [uBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com/).
